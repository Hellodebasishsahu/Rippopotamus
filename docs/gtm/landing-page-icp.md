# Landing page copy (ICP: editors & designers who download from gated sources)

## Hero

**Headline:** If you can see it, you can save it.

**Sub:** Rippopotamus rides your own browser session to pull full-quality video and audio from the places that block everything else—your Instagram, Facebook, Drive, members-only boards—straight into editor-ready project folders. All local.

## Bullets

- Reach anything you're logged into: own IG/FB/Drive, members-only posts, private client boards (your browser session, never your password)
- Full-quality 4K/8K video + master audio that browser extensions can't touch
- Editor-ready `Source/Audio/Images` folders with sane filenames + `manifest.json`
- Local-first: links, downloads, and credentials never leave your machine
- macOS now; Windows in progress

## Secondary feature (not the headline)

- **Tracker import:** teams running projects from a Google Sheet can turn a tracker tab into organized project folders + manifest. Surfaced as a discoverable in-app utility, not the core pitch.

## CTA

**Primary:** Download for macOS.
**Secondary:** Book a 20-min walkthrough.

## Avoid

- “AI video search” or semantic promises until the product ships it.
- Leading with the spreadsheet workflow—it's a feature, not the positioning.
