# ICP discovery script (12–15 calls)

**Audience:** Producer + lead editor at small branded/corporate video teams using **Google Sheets + Drive** and local Premiere/Resolve/FCP.

## Opener

“We’re building Rippopotamus to turn tracker sheets and Drive links into editor-ready project folders with manifests—not another generic downloader.”

## Core questions

1. Walk me through the last project: where do links live before editors download?
2. How long does intake take per project? What breaks most often (login, duplicates, wrong filenames)?
3. When someone re-downloads the same Drive file, how do you notice?
4. What would “done” look like for handoff from producer to editor?

## Pass / fail

- **Pass:** Sheet is source of truth; pain on intake > 30 min/month; cares about local files + client confidentiality.
- **Fail:** Cloud-only DAM, no sheets, or only one-off downloads.
